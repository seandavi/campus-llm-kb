# Strategic Reframing: From LLMs to Agentic AI

This document outlines the proposed pivot for the knowledge base from a focus on "Large Language Models" (LLMs) to a focus on **Agentic AI and Autonomous Systems** within the Academic Medical Center (AMC).

## 1. The Core Thesis Shift
*   **Current State:** LLMs as assistive tools (summarization, drafting, search).
*   **Future State:** AI Agents as collaborative "digital residents" (task execution, loop-closing, multi-tool orchestration).
*   **Impact:** The framework must move from "Data Governance" to "Systemic Agency & Safety."

## 2. Recommended Chapter Additions & Gaps
*   **Evaluation & Monitoring (The "Flight Recorder"):** Critical for 2026. Moves beyond benchmarks to real-world drift detection and "shadow deployment."
*   **Agentic Safety & Guardrails:** Specific chapter on "Human-in-the-Loop" (HITL) requirements and the "kill-switch" architecture for autonomous clinical workflows.
*   **Patient & Community Trust:** Establishing Patient AI Advisory Councils to maintain the social license for agentic intervention.
*   **Human-Factors & UX:** How clinicians interact with agents without developing "automation bias" or cognitive load from too many notifications.
*   **Professional Wellness (The "Ambient" Win):** Using agents to eliminate "pajama time" through ambient documentation and automated inbox management.
*   **Library & Information Science:** The evolution of medical libraries into "Knowledge Graph" and "RAG Hub" providers for institutional agents.

## 3. Structural Reframing
*   **Rename Repository:** Suggest `campus-ai-orchestration` or `agentic-academic-medicine`.
*   **Rename Book:** "AI Orchestration in Academic Medicine: A Framework for Agentic Systems and Governance."
*   **Update Part 2 (Implementation):** Shift focus from "How to prompt" to "How to integrate APIs and verify agentic loops."

## 4. Workstream Updates
*   **Data Use & Access** → **Environment & Tools:** Agents need access to EHR APIs, not just static datasets.
*   **IT & Infrastructure** → **Agentic Infrastructure:** Focus on API gateways, vector databases, and inference orchestration.
*   **PM & Support** → **Operational Governance:** Managing the lifecycle of an active digital agent vs. a static software tool.
